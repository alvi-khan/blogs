# Blog posts I've been reading recently

- [What to care about in a job](https://www.benkuhn.net/job2/): General advice on things to look for in your work life.
- [Be impatient](https://www.benkuhn.net/impatient/): Argues in favor of being impatient with everthing, giving examples of cases where impatience led to efficiency and innovation.
- [Rest in motion](https://mindingourway.com/rest-in-motion/): Argues that the default state for human beings is not rest, but motion (or rather, work). To be clear, the article does not advocate for workaholism.
- [Staring into the abyss as a core life skill](https://www.benkuhn.net/abyss/): Advocates for thinking about uncomfortable thoughts, thereby forcing ourselves to make difficult decisions that ultimately benefit us.
- [To listen well, get curious](https://www.benkuhn.net/listen/): Clarifies on common advice about being a good listener by re-inforcing that the primary thing to focus on is being curious about the person you're listening to and their experience.
- [Some mistakes I made as a new manager](https://www.benkuhn.net/newmgr/): Advice for people just getting into a management role. Also indirectly provides guidance on the mentality to have towards your co-workers.
- [Why and how to write things on the Internet](https://www.benkuhn.net/writing/): Encouragement to start writing blog posts.
- [Blameful Post-Mortems](https://staysaasy.com/saas/2025/03/11/blameful.html): Discusses the nuances of a blameless culture and how to maintain balance between unfair punishments and lack of accountability.
- [Your Manager Is Not Your Best Friend](https://staysaasy.com/management/2025/06/01/your-manager-is-not-your-best-friend.html): Warns of the harms of complaining publicly about colleagues and gives advice to managers on how to handle complaints from reports.
